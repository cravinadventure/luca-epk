LUCA

DJ + Immersive Experience Creator
About

The face of The Field.

LUCA is a DJ and immersive experience creator, and the character at the center of LUCA IMMERSIVE: a platform for multi-sensory live experiences, immersive installations, and a growing narrative world. The work blends visual storytelling, music, and interactive technology to transport audiences into new realities.

The name means bringer of light, from the Latin lux and Greek leukós: the physical light of projection and LEDs, and the inner light of awareness. It is also the Last Universal Common Ancestor, the single root all life branches from, the same way every idea branches from The Field. In the story, LUCA is that light given a form, a guide who reveals imagination and hands it to the people in the room.
