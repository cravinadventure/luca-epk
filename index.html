About

The face of The Field.

LUCA is a DJ and immersive experience creator, and the character at the center of LUCA IMMERSIVE: a platform for multi-sensory live experiences, immersive installations, and a growing narrative world.

The name means bringer of light, from the Latin lux and Greek leukós. It is also the Last Universal Common Ancestor, the single root all life branches from, the same way every idea branches from The Field. In the story, LUCA is that light given a form.

The Work

Experiences, not just sets.

Immersive live experiences where the crowd's own light becomes the visuals. Real-time projection mapping, interactive tech, and DJ sets built as one reactive, evolving environment.
